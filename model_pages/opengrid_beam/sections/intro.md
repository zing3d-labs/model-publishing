This beam combines the openGrid connector with a structural beam element, allowing you to join two openGrid tiles at a 90-degree angle. Use beams to build baskets, shelving, enclosures, or more complex modular structures.

Available in both Full (6.8mm) and Lite (4mm) grid thicknesses, with lengths ranging from 2 to 8 units. Use the OpenSCAD customizer to generate any size you need.
