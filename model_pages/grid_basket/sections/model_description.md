A parametric snap-together storage basket designed to mount directly to any openGrid panel. Build wall-mounted shelves, desk organizers, or modular storage systems by attaching baskets to your openGrid grid. Disconnect the basket from your wall grid and take it with you for an easy way to carry your stuff. Snap it back on to a grid on another wall. The uses are endless!

Each basket is made up of interlocking grid-patterned wall panels and corner beams that print flat and snap together without tools or hardware. The package includes dual-sided snap connectors for secure attachment to either Full (6.8mm) and Lite (3.4mm) openGrid tiles.

Size is fully configurable in 28mm openGrid units — choose from the provided presets or customize to any X×Y×Z dimension.
