## Assembly

All parts print flat as provided by the pre-made profiles or customizer. No supports required.

1. **Print** — Use the provided STL or 3MF.
2. **Assemble the frame** — Press the grid wall panels into the corner beams. They snap together.
3. **Attach the snaps** — Press the dual-sided snap connectors into the back wall or base of the assembled basket, depending on your desired usage. For wall mounted basket with directional snaps, install the snap with the arrow pointing down near the top of the basket, inserting the arrow side first and pushing the other end once the arrow is in place. The other side of the dual snap should have the arrow pointing up. If using multiple dual sided snaps on the same column, the lower one should be non-directional so it can push directly in.
4. **Mount** — Snap the basket onto any openGrid tile. For directional snaps, the directional arrow should be facing up and near the top of the basket's wall. Angle the basket/snap so that the arrow goes in first, then push the rest of the snap into place. To remove, pull out from the bottom and reverse the process. For non-directional snaps, press straight in until the snaps engage.
