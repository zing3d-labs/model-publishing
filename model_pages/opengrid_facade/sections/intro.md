A flat facade panel that snaps onto openGrid tiles, giving a clean finished look to baskets, walls, or enclosures built with openGrid. Features configurable corner rounding or chamfering, adjustable snap placement, and optional removal notches for easy disassembly.

Designed for Lite (3.4mm) openGrid tiles, available in all sizes from 2x2 to 9x9 units. Use the OpenSCAD customizer to generate any size you need.

## Snap Recommendations

For most uses, corner-only non-directional snaps are all you need. The facade is not a structural part -- it's either a vertical piece for aesthetics or a horizontal surface filling openings in a grid. In horizontal use, items resting on the facade hold it in place with gravity, so the extra hold of directional snaps isn't necessary.

One useful approach: use Lite snaps on the facade. If you attach it to a standard (Full) grid, there is usually enough room on the other side of the grid to use another Lite snap and attach something else to the same grid spot.

Additional snap placement options (edges, all) are available if your use case calls for them.
